About StarBlue

StarBlue is a simulated hedge fund project designed to let you practice portfolio management with a true institutional aesthetic. Start with $100 cash and enter transactions — buys, sells, and price updates — to see how a real hedge fund's portfolio value evolves over time.

Use this as a sandbox for strategy testing, pitch visuals, or as a learning tool. The interface emphasizes clarity, fast decision logging, and an elegant presentation — just like a high-performance hedge fund.
